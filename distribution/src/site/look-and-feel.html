Look and Feel

Swing supports a look and feel mechanism. Swiby is going to support Swing's standard look and feel capability most likely via simplified mechanism:

  require 'foobar_lnf'

It can make Swing applications look like Windows or Mac OS X and a whole array of less standard and more stylized designs:
