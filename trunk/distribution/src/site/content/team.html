Committers

PicoContainer committers introduce themselves in their own words and are listed in orde of arrival.

Jean Lazarou

Jean first demonstrated Swiby in January 2007, writing it in pure JRuby. He modelled it initially after JavaFX (then known as 'F3') but knew that there's competitors in Adobe's Flash/Flex/AIR as well as Microsoft's Silverlight.

Paul Hammant

Paul works for ThoughtWorks in the USA. He's been interested in a builder like language leveraging Swing for web usage for a number of years. In 2004, he started 'Thicky' but is happier with JRuby intead of Groovy, hence that's not developed anymore, and he's on Jean's Swiby project.
